About AvyRisk

AvyRisk is a powerful and innovative app designed to enhance your mountain adventures. Our mission is to provide you with the tools and information you need to make informed decisions and stay safe while exploring the great outdoors.

Our app leverages cutting-edge technology to assess the risk of avalanches and other mountain-related dangers. By analyzing factors such as temperature, wind, fresh snowfall, slope steepness, and slope direction, AvyRisk calculates a danger score that helps you understand the potential hazards of your chosen route.

Whether you're an experienced mountaineer or a novice adventurer, AvyRisk is your ultimate companion for risk assessment and safety. Prioritize your well-being and embark on your mountain journeys with confidence.

Features

Accurate Danger Score Calculation

Our cutting-edge technology analyzes various factors to provide you with an accurate danger score for your chosen route.

Real-time Weather Data Integration

Stay informed with real-time weather data integration, helping you make informed decisions based on the latest conditions.

Expert Tips and Recommendations

Receive expert tips and recommendations to navigate mountain hazards safely and enjoy your adventure.

User-friendly Interface

Our intuitive user interface makes it easy for both experienced mountaineers and novices to use the app effectively.
